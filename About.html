Welcome!

We are an group of avid developers tasked with the challenge to create an app that solves problems and is user-friendly. Trips to Plan is a web application that does just that! Created by Michael McVeigh, Aaron Moskowitz, Tony Brown, and Shamique Thompson, this app is intended to simplify the process of planning a vacation around your interests.
